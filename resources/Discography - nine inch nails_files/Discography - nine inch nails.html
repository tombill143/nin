    The Fragile [1999]

    Closure [1997]

    Part live artifact, part fever dream.  Nine Inch Nails spent a lot of money to properly film shows from The Downward Spiral tour cycle, but threw the project away when Reznor said the footage “looked like shit – it was too professional.”  The “proper” tour document became this assemblage of Hi-8 footage filmed by Jonathan Rach.  It captures “a much truer sense of what happened” according to Reznor.  Currently out of print.
